Internet Topics

The Internet is made up of many independly operated networks. There is no one central node or center that controls it. Many people don't know who invented the Internet or how it works or even ask just as how we don't normally ask who invented the pen or the zipper. But the Internet was created by Vint Cerf and Bob Khan, which was based on the ARPANET (invented by Paul Baran). What Cerf and Khan did was create a nationwide packet switching network. It took the ARPANET, the packet switching network previously available to researchers and the government and made that publicly available nationwide, and eventually globally.
